# Lokvani-LokVaniAI – Smart Civic Complaint Management System
Project Description
LokVaniAI is a civic complaint management platform designed to connect citizens directly with government authorities and municipal departments. The platform enables people to report issues in their locality quickly and efficiently, helping authorities identify and resolve community problems in a timely manner.
Citizens can submit complaints related to various public infrastructure and civic issues, such as:
Potholes and damaged roads
Drainage and sewage problems
Water supply issues
Streetlight failures
Waste management concerns
Road accidents and public safety hazards
Other local community problems
Users can upload images of the issue along with a brief description and the address of the location where the problem exists. This helps authorities understand the issue clearly and take appropriate action.
Once submitted, the complaint is forwarded to the concerned department or authority responsible for resolving the problem.
Key Features
📷 Image-Based Complaint Reporting
Citizens can capture and upload images of issues directly from their mobile devices, providing visual evidence of the problem.
📝 Complaint Description
Users can provide a short description explaining the issue, making it easier for authorities to understand the situation.
📍 Address Submission
Citizens can enter the exact address or location details of the issue to help the concerned department identify the affected area accurately.
📊 Government Monitoring Dashboard
Officials can access a centralized dashboard to:
View registered complaints
Track complaint status
Monitor reported issues
Manage and update resolution progress
Objectives
Provide a simple and accessible platform for reporting civic issues.
Improve communication between citizens and government authorities.
Enable quicker identification and resolution of community problems.
Increase transparency in the complaint handling process.
Encourage active citizen participation in improving local communities.
Expected Impact
LokVaniAI helps create safer, cleaner, and better-maintained communities by enabling citizens to directly report local issues. The platform supports efficient complaint management and assists authorities in responding to public concerns more effectively.**I
